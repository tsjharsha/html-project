Pushpa


Story

Pushpa is a 2021 Indian Telugu-language action thriller film directed by Sukumar. The film follows the story of Pushpa Raj, a red sandalwood smuggler, and his rise to power in the forest regions of Andhra Pradesh.

Cast

  • Allu Arjun as Pushpa Raj
  • Rashmika Mandanna as Srivalli
  • Fahadh Faasil as Bhanwar Singh Shekhawat
  • Jagapathi Babu as Veerendra Raj
  • Prakash Raj as Jolly Reddy
