Chatrapathi


Story

Chatrapathi is a 2005 Indian Telugu-language action drama film directed by S.S. Rajamouli. The film follows the story of Shivaji, who becomes a powerful leader and fights against injustice after being separated from his family in childhood.

Cast

  • Prabhas as Shivaji
  • Shriya Saran as Neelu
  • Bhanupriya as Shivaji's Mother
  • Pradeep Rawat as Suleiman Seth
  • Gundu Hanumantha Rao as Neelu's Father
