RRR


Story

RRR is an Indian Telugu-language period action film directed by S.S. Rajamouli. The film narrates a fictional story revolving around India's freedom fighters, Alluri Sitarama Raju and Komaram Bheem, who fought against the British Raj and the Nizam of Hyderabad, respectively.

Cast

  • N. T. Rama Rao Jr. as Komaram Bheem
  • Ram Charan as Alluri Sitarama Raju
  • Alia Bhatt as Sita
  • Ajay Devgn as Mentor of Alluri Sitarama Raju
  • Olivia Morris as Jennifer
