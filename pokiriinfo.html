Pokiri


Story

Pokiri is a 2006 Indian Telugu-language action thriller film directed by Puri Jagannadh. The film revolves around a fearless young man, Pandu, who is hired by a police officer to infiltrate a mafia gang led by a notorious underworld don.

Cast

  • Mahesh Babu as Pandu
  • Ileana D'Cruz as Shruti
  • Prakash Raj as Ali Bhai
  • Ashish Vidyarthi as ACP Satyamoorthy
  • Nassar as Home Minister
  • Brahmanandam as Brahmi
