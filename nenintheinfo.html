Neninthe


Story

Neninthe is a 2008 Indian Telugu-language action drama film directed by Puri Jagannadh. The film portrays the struggles and aspirations of a young aspiring director in the Telugu film industry.

Cast

  • Ravi Teja as Ravi
  • Siya Gautham as Sandhya
  • Mumaith Khan as herself
  • Brahmanandam as Lucky
  • Supreet as Bada
